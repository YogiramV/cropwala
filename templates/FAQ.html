Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our farming app

What does this app do for me as a farmer?
+
This app helps you choose the best crop to grow based on your soil, weather, and season. It also recommends the right fertilizers and updates you on new products, pest issues, and weather alerts — all in one place.
How do I know which crop is best for my land?
+
You can enter your soil details, current weather, and the time of year into the app. It uses smart technology to suggest the best crops based on this data and local farming trends.
Is this app available in my local language?
+
Yes, the app supports multiple languages, including Tamil. You can choose your preferred language when you first open the app.
Can I trust the fertilizer suggestions given by the app?
+
Yes, the app uses verified data from agriculture experts and government databases. It also includes ratings and reviews from other farmers to help you choose the most effective fertilizer.
What if I don't understand how to use the app?
+
The app includes a voice-based assistant (chatbot) that can help guide you through every step — you can ask questions and get answers in your language, either by speaking or typing.
Does the app work without the internet?
+
Some features like weather updates and crop suggestions need the internet. However, basic information and past recommendations can be accessed offline once loaded.
Is there any cost to use this app?
+
No, the core features like crop suggestion, fertilizer info, and weather alerts are completely free to use. Additional marketplace or expert consultation features may have optional costs in the future.
